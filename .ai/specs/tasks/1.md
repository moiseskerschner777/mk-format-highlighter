**Goal:** `MkComment` and `MkCommentManager` exist and correctly capture a selection as a comment, provable via a debug log dump — no UI yet.

**Flow:** Flow 1 — Build→Observe→Adjust

**References:** `design.md` → Component contracts (`MkComment`, `MkCommentManager`)
